The Best Movies According To Sricharan

Top Three Movies Of All Time

1. Baahubali: The Conclusion (2017)⚔️

- Directed by S.S. Rajamouli
- Prabhas played dual roles: Amarendra Baahubali and Mahendra Baahubali
- Became the highest-grossing Indian film at the time
- Known for its epic scale, emotional depth, and the iconic question: “Why did Kattappa kill Baahubali?”
- Cemented Prabhas as a pan-Indian superstar

2. Baahubali: The Beginning (2015)⚔️

- The film that introduced the world of Mahishmati
- Prabhas’s portrayal of Shivudu and Baahubali was both heroic and heartfelt
- Set new benchmarks in Indian cinema for visual effects and storytelling
- Laid the foundation for the sequel’s massive success

3. Salaar: Part 1 – Ceasefire (2023)🔥

- Directed by Prashanth Neel
- Prabhas played Deva, a rugged and mysterious warrior
- Praised for its raw action, intense visuals, and gripping narrative
- Marked a powerful return to mass-action cinema for Prabhas
